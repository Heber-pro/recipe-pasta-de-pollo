Guatemalan Pasta de pollo

Pasta de pollo is basically the guatemalan chicken salad It's a popular creamy chicken salad that is a staple at parties and gatherings often served with crackers or tostadas

It differs from standard American chicken salad by featuring a mixture of various ingredients.

Ingredients
  • Protein:
    1. Shredded chicken breast
    2. Rotisseri chicken
  • Vegetables:
    1. Boiled and diced potatoes
    2. Boiled and diced carrot
    3. Frozen or canned peas
    4. canned or fresh sweet corn
    5. Chopped pickled jalapenos
  • Dressing:
    1. Mayonnaise
    2. Sour cream
    3. Pickled jalapeno optional
    4. Salt and pepper to taste
    5. Garlic Podwer optional
  • Preparation:
    1. You need a bowl
    2. Mix very well until we incorporate all the ingredients
    3. you salt
      4. It can be enjoyed as a sandwich, with tostadas or as you prefer
